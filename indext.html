Abrief time line of Elon's life:

  • 1971 - Born in south African
  • 1980 -After his parents divorced, he lived for 9 years with his father in Pretoria until deciding to move to Canada, your mother's born country, intending to migrate to the USA in the future.
  • 1997 - graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in economics from the Wharton School and a Bachelor of Arts degree in physics from the College of Arts and Sciences.
  • 1995 - Founded Zip2 with his brother Kimbal and Greg Kouri, the company developed and marketed an internet city guide for the newspaper publishing industry, with maps, directions and yellow pages.
  • 1999 - Co-founded X.com, the first online payment method that became PayPal and sold to eBay in 2002 for U$1.5 billion.
  • 2001 - Attempting to bring back the attention to space exploration, Musk founded Space X dreaming about transform humans into a multi-planetary civilization. Space X main achievement so far is the Falcon rockets with the ability to land after the launch, being reusable.
  • 2004 - Got involved is Tesla Inc., after some conflicts and the financial crisis in 2008 Martin Eberhard was ousted and Elon assumed the CEO position.
  • -- Many other great projects had his involvement like the Solar city (2006), Hyperloop (2013), OpenAI (2015), NeuroLink (2016).
